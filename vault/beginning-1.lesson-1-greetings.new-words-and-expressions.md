> Syllables carry meaning. Conjoining them can also create words with new meanings sometimes.  
> - 일학년 - Freshman (school) year
> - 이학년 - Sophomore (school) year
> - 삼학년 - Junior (school) year
> - 사학년 - Senior (school) year
